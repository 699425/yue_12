The Chinese Zodiac

What is it?

The Chinese zodiac is a repeating 12-year cycle, with each year associated with an animal sign. The 12 animal signs are Rat, Ox, Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon, Snake, Horse, Goat, Monkey, Rooster, Dog and Pig.

History

The Chinese zodiac has ancient origins, dating back over 2,000 years. It is based on the lunar calendar and is deeply rooted in Chinese culture and mythology.
